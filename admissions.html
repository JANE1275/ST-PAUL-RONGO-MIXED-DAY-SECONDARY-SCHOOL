Our Admissions Process

We make the application process simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to join our school community.

1

Inquiry

Submit an inquiry form to begin the process and receive our information package

2

School Tour

Schedule a campus visit to experience our facilities and meet our staff

3

Application

Complete the application package with all required documents

4

Admission

Receive your admissions decision and enrollment information

Admissions Requirements

To ensure a smooth application process, please prepare the following documents:

Application Form

Dully filled application form with recent passport photo

Academic Records

Certified copies of previous school reports and KCPE results

Entrance Exam

Completed entrance examination (for Form 1 applicants)

Parent Meeting

Interview with parents/guardians and student

Important Dates & Deadlines

Mark your calendar with these key dates for the 2025 admissions cycle:

January 15

March 1

Entrance examinations

Application deadline
